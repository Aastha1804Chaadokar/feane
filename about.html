We Are Feane

Hello! We are Feane, a fast food restaurant that opened in 2010. We started as a small shop and now we have grown into a full restaurant that serves hundreds of customers every day.

Our main goal is simple - we want to give you the best food at a price that is fair. We use fresh vegetables and good quality meat every single day. Nothing is frozen or stored for too long.

We have a team of 10 chefs who all love cooking. They each bring their own style and ideas to our menu. That is why our food always tastes different and exciting.
